# Tutta — Product Vision

## What is Tutta?

Tutta is a mobile-first rental marketplace that connects guests and hosts for **short-term stays ranging from 1 day to 1 month**. Unlike traditional long-term rental platforms, Tutta focuses on flexibility and affordability for short stays in homes and rooms, with daily, weekly, and monthly pricing options.

Tutta also introduces a unique **skill-exchange / free stay** model as a premium host feature, where hosts can offer accommodation in return for valuable skills — such as English tutoring, content creation, graphic design, or general assistance — instead of monetary payment.

---

## Target Users

### Guests
- Travelers and tourists looking for affordable, flexible accommodation
- Professionals on short business trips or contract assignments
- Students and interns relocating temporarily
- Remote workers seeking month-long stays in new cities
- Individuals in transition (e.g., between apartments)

### Hosts
- Homeowners or renters with spare rooms or vacant properties
- Individuals looking to monetize unused space on a flexible schedule
- Premium hosts who want to attract guests through skill exchange rather than price

---

## Main Value Proposition

| Audience | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Guests** | Find and book flexible, short-term stays (1 day – 1 month) easily and securely, with transparent pricing and direct communication with hosts. |
| **Hosts** | List properties quickly, manage bookings from a mobile app, receive payments through local payment systems, and optionally offer skill-exchange stays for free. |
| **Platform** | A trustworthy marketplace with verified users, safe in-app chat, review system, and local payment integrations (Click, Payme). |

**Core differentiators:**
- Flexible duration: daily, weekly, and monthly pricing in a single listing
- Skill-exchange / free stay option for premium hosts
- Built for local markets with local payment providers integrated from day one
- Simple, mobile-first experience for both guests and hosts

---

## MVP Scope

The MVP will deliver the minimum set of features needed to validate core guest and host workflows on the mobile platform.

### In Scope

#### Authentication
- Guest and host sign-up and login (JWT-based)
- Basic profile creation

#### Listings
- Hosts can create, edit, and delete listings
- Upload photos
- Add location, property type, and description
- Set daily, weekly, and monthly prices
- Listings displayed on a searchable list and map view

#### Search & Discovery
- Search listings by location and dates
- Filter by price range, property type, and duration

#### Booking
- Guests can select dates and send a booking request
- Hosts can accept or decline bookings
- Basic booking status tracking (pending / confirmed / cancelled)

#### Payments
- Integration with Click and Payme for in-app payments

#### Communication
- In-app chat between guest and host after a booking is initiated

#### Reviews
- Guests can leave a review after a completed stay

### Out of Scope for MVP
- Skill-exchange / free stay listings (premium feature — post-MVP)
- OneID authentication integration
- Advanced analytics or host dashboard
- Push notifications
- Admin panel / moderation tools
- Web version of the platform
