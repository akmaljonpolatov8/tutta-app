# Product Vision

## Problem
Many travelers need affordable and flexible accommodations for short stays.

## Target Users
- Travelers looking for short-term rentals
- Homeowners wanting to monetize their spaces

## Value Proposition
Tutta provides a simple, user-friendly platform to connect homeowners with short-term guests.

## Principles
- User-centric design
- Transparency in listings and fees
- Quick response times for support

## Success Metrics
- Number of successful bookings
- User retention rates
- Customer satisfaction ratings

## Non-Goals
- Competing with long-term rental services
- Extensive geographical coverage initially.
